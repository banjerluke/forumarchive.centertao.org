my daily, cyclic prayer

okietaoist
November 2011 edited November 2011 in Taoism and the Tao Te Ching
from the tao came one.
with that one, two.
with that two, three.
from that three, ten thousand things.

i am one of those ten thousand things,
i am one with those ten thousand things
as we ten thousand things are one with the tao.
